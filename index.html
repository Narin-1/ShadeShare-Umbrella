About Us

ShadeShare was born from a simple observation: many people are often caught in sudden rains. This results in either time lost spent waiting for the rain to subside, risk sickness by running in the rain or a waste in resources when resorting to buy an umbrella on the spot. ShadeShare is charged by a dymanic group of entrepreneurs, technologists and problem solvers who are passionate about making everyday life more convenient and sustainable for everyone.



"In the sharing economy, access is the new ownership. It's not about owning the assest, but about assessing its value when you need it."

Lisa Gansky, entrepreneur and author
